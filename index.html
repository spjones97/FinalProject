Welcome to COMP 455

This course will be an introduction to the theory of computation. Finite automata, regular languages, pushdown automata, context-free languages, Turing machines, and Undecidable problems. This course will consist of two mid-semester exams, a final, and probably 9 homework assignments. The midsemester exams will be on Tuesday, September 24 and Thursday, October 31. The final exam is at noon on Thursday, December 12. Exams are in class and are closed book and closed notes. All quizzes together count one percent of the grade. Other than this, the grading algorithm will probably be 36 percent for the final exam, 25 percent for the first mid semester exam, 26 percent for the second mid semester exam, and 12 percent for homework. There may also be a few extra credit challenge problems.
